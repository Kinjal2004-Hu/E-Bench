CASE: Fatal Road Accident — Rash and Negligent Driving

FIR NO: 234/2026
POLICE STATION: Koramangala Traffic Police Station, Bengaluru

COMPLAINANT: Mr. Suresh Naik, Age 45, Father of the deceased

DECEASED: Mr. Aditya Naik, Age 22, Student at RV College of Engineering, Bengaluru

ACCUSED: Mr. Dheeraj Gowda, Age 27, Driver of the vehicle (White Toyota Fortuner, Registration: KA-01-MH-4521)

DATE & TIME OF INCIDENT: 8th February 2026, approximately 11:45 PM

PLACE OF OCCURRENCE: 80 Feet Road, Koramangala 4th Block, near Sony Signal Junction, Bengaluru

FACTS OF THE CASE:

On the night of 8th February 2026 at approximately 11:45 PM, Mr. Aditya Naik (deceased, age 22) was crossing 80 Feet Road at the designated pedestrian crossing near Sony Signal Junction in Koramangala 4th Block, Bengaluru. The pedestrian signal was green at the time.

The accused Mr. Dheeraj Gowda was driving a White Toyota Fortuner (KA-01-MH-4521) at an estimated speed of 110-120 km/h in a 40 km/h speed limit zone. The accused jumped the red traffic signal and struck the deceased with full force.

The impact threw the deceased approximately 25 feet from the point of collision. The deceased suffered severe head trauma, multiple fractures to the ribcage, and internal bleeding. Bystanders immediately called 108 ambulance service.

The deceased was rushed to St. John's Medical College Hospital where he was declared brought dead at 12:30 AM on 9th February 2026.

INVESTIGATION FINDINGS:

1. CCTV footage from nearby commercial establishment clearly shows the accused's vehicle running the red light at high speed. The footage has been secured by police.

2. Blood alcohol test conducted on the accused at 1:15 AM showed BAC (Blood Alcohol Content) of 0.14%, which is significantly above the legal limit of 0.03%.

3. The accused had 3 prior traffic violation challans for overspeeding and 1 prior challan for drunk driving (September 2025).

4. The vehicle's speedometer, upon forensic examination, was found stuck at 118 km/h.

5. The accused attempted to flee the scene but was apprehended by traffic police at a checkpoint 2 km away.

6. Two empty liquor bottles (Blenders Pride whisky) were found in the vehicle.

7. The accused does not possess a valid driving license — his license was suspended in October 2025 following the drunk driving incident.

WITNESSES:
1. Mr. Karthik Reddy (Auto driver) — eyewitness at the signal
2. Mrs. Padmini Rao (Pedestrian) — saw the accident happen
3. Mr. Imran Khan (Shop owner) — provided CCTV footage
4. Dr. Anand Kumar (Emergency physician) — confirmed death on arrival

MEDICAL REPORT:
- Cause of death: Severe craniocerebral injuries with subdural hemorrhage
- Multiple rib fractures (7 ribs broken)
- Ruptured spleen and liver laceration
- Compound fracture of left femur

COMPENSATION SOUGHT:
The family seeks compensation of Rs. 1,50,00,000 considering the deceased was a final year engineering student with excellent academic record and confirmed placement at Infosys with annual package of Rs. 12,00,000.
