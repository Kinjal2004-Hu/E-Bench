CASE: Domestic Violence and Dowry Harassment

COMPLAINANT: Mrs. Priya Verma, Age 28, currently residing at her parental home in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh

ACCUSED:
1. Mr. Vikram Verma (Husband), Age 32
2. Mrs. Sunita Verma (Mother-in-law), Age 58
3. Mr. Harish Verma (Father-in-law), Age 62

FACTS OF THE CASE:

Mrs. Priya Verma was married to Mr. Vikram Verma on 12th March 2024 in Lucknow as per Hindu rites and ceremonies. At the time of marriage, Mrs. Priya's parents gave dowry including gold jewellery worth Rs. 8,00,000, a Maruti Suzuki Swift car worth Rs. 8,50,000, household items worth Rs. 3,00,000, and cash of Rs. 5,00,000.

Within three months of the marriage, the accused persons began demanding additional dowry of Rs. 15,00,000 for purchasing a flat in Noida. When the complainant's parents expressed their inability to meet such demands, the harassment intensified.

SPECIFIC INCIDENTS OF CRUELTY:

1. June 2024: Mr. Vikram Verma physically assaulted the complainant, causing bruises on her arms and back. Mrs. Sunita Verma verbally abused the complainant, calling her family "beggars" and threatening to get her son remarried.

2. August 2024: The complainant was denied food for two days when her parents failed to transfer Rs. 2,00,000 demanded by the in-laws. She was locked in a room and her mobile phone was confiscated.

3. October 2024: Mr. Vikram Verma threw hot chai on the complainant's face, causing minor burns. When the complainant threatened to call the police, the accused threatened to file false cases against her family.

4. December 2024: The complainant was forced to sign blank cheques and property papers. She was threatened with acid attack if she refused.

5. February 2025: The complainant was physically thrown out of the matrimonial home at 11 PM with only the clothes she was wearing. She managed to reach her parental home with help from a neighbor.

MEDICAL EVIDENCE:
- MLC (Medico-Legal Certificate) from District Hospital, Noida dated 15th October 2024 documenting burn injuries
- Psychological evaluation report from NIMHANS documenting severe anxiety and PTSD
- Photographs of physical injuries taken at various dates

WITNESSES:
1. Mrs. Kavita Saxena (Neighbor) - witnessed the complainant being thrown out
2. Mr. Arun Verma (Husband's cousin) - witnessed dowry demands during family gatherings
3. Dr. Meena Gupta - treating physician who documented injuries

RELIEF SOUGHT:
1. Protection order under Domestic Violence Act
2. Monthly maintenance of Rs. 50,000
3. Return of stridhan (gold jewellery and personal belongings)
4. Compensation of Rs. 10,00,000 for physical and mental cruelty
5. Criminal prosecution of all accused under relevant sections of BNS and Dowry Prohibition Act
