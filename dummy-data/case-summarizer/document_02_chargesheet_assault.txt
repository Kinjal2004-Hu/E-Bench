TO THE HON'BLE COURT:

The investigation of the above-mentioned case has been completed and this chargesheet is being filed under Section 193 of Bharatiya Nagarik Suraksha Sanhita (BNSS), 2023.

BRIEF FACTS:

On 15th October 2025 at approximately 9:30 PM, the victim Mr. Ramvilas Meena was returning home from his shop (Meena Kirana Store) at Tonk Road, Jaipur. Near Jyoti Circle, Malviya Nagar, the victim was accosted by accused No. 1 (Mr. Manoj Kumar Saini) and accused No. 2 (Mr. Deepak Sharma).

An altercation ensued regarding a money lending dispute. The victim had borrowed Rs. 3,00,000 from accused No. 1 at an interest rate of 5% per month (60% per annum), which is usurious and illegal. When the victim was unable to repay the principal along with the exorbitant interest totaling Rs. 5,40,000, the accused persons turned violent.

Accused No. 1 struck the victim with an iron rod on his head and back, causing grievous injuries. Accused No. 2 held the victim down and kicked him repeatedly. The victim sustained:
- Fracture of the left parietal bone (skull)
- Subdural hematoma
- Fractures of the 4th, 5th, and 6th ribs on the left side
- Contusions and abrasions on the back, arms, and legs

The victim was left unconscious at the scene. A passerby, Mr. Govind Prasad (PW-3), discovered the victim at approximately 10:15 PM and called 112 emergency services.

ROLE OF ACCUSED NO. 3 (MS. SUNITA DEVI):
Investigation has revealed that accused No. 3, wife of accused No. 1, was present at the scene and instigated the attack. Call records show she contacted accused No. 2 at 8:45 PM on the day of the incident and directed him to "teach a lesson" to the victim. She was also found to have maintained the illegal money lending accounts and collected interest payments.
